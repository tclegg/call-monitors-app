Monitors by Lead

This page will show all the monitors completed by the selected lead.

Agent Maintenance

Add/Remove/Change agents. Agents that are inactive will not show up in the monitored agents list, but will show up in the completed monitors. To toggle the active state of the agent use the / button to the right of the agent's row.

Lead Maintenance

Add/Remove/Change leads. Leads that are inactive will not show up in the New Monitors form, but will be in the Monitors by Lead form. Toggle the active state of the lead by clicking the / button to the right of the lead.
